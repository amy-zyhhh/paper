## 摘要

<p>In this paper, critical loads for cylindrical-bending instability of simply supported wide plates are calculated analytically without any simplifying assumptions on the through-thickness variations of displacements, and without any limitations on the plates’ thickness-to-length ratios (for thick and thin plates). These calculations are performed for an isotropic, linearly elastic material, and are used to evaluate accuracy of critical loads produced by engineering plate theories, for large range of thickness-to-length ratios. Additionally, the obtained analytical solution is used to evaluate accuracy of critical load calculated by the MSC Nastran finite element analysis software with the use of plain strain elements.</p>

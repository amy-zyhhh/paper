## 摘要

<p>The influence of combined radial pressure and axial compression on the vibration characteristics, circumferential mode shapes, and stability of thin-walled circular cylindrical shells is investigated within a consistent Flügge–Lur’e–Byrne shell formulation. The governing equations are linearized about a prestressed equilibrium configuration, enabling a unified treatment of vibration and buckling within a single eigenvalue framework. The study demonstrates that radial and axial prestresses affect shell dynamics through fundamentally different physical mechanisms. Radial pressure induces pronounced stiffness softening accompanied by significant distortion of circumferential mode shapes, whereas axial compression primarily modifies natural frequency magnitudes with comparatively minor influence on modal distributions. To capture these effects, a semi-analytical differential transform method (DTM) is employed, which enables direct construction of continuous two-dimensional circumferential mode shapes for prescribed circumferential wave numbers, without spatial discretization and while retaining full displacement coupling. This capability allows systematic tracking of mode-shape evolution under increasing prestress, revealing features that cannot be inferred from frequency variation alone. The onset of instability is identified by the vanishing of the lowest natural frequency, providing a physically transparent criterion for determining critical buckling loads under both radial and axial prestresses. The predicted vibration characteristics, mode shapes, and buckling loads show excellent agreement with established analytical and numerical results. The findings provide new mechanics-based insight into prestress-induced vibration–stability interactions in cylindrical shells and are directly relevant to the design and stability assessment of thin-walled shell structures.</p>

<h3>主要结论</h3>
<ul><li>径向与轴向预应力通过不同的物理机制影响壳体动力学：径向压力导致显著的刚度软化并伴随周向模态形状的严重畸变；而轴向压缩主要改变固有频率大小，对模态分布的影响相对较小。</li>
<li>追踪预应力增加时的模态演化至关重要，能揭示仅靠频率分析无法获得的结构行为特征。</li>
<li>在统一的特征值框架内，通过最低固有频率消失来判定失稳起始，为径向和轴向预应力下的临界屈曲载荷提供了一个物理意义清晰的判据。</li></ul>
<h3>亮点和创新点</h3>
<ul><li>在Flügge–Lur’e–Byrne理论框架内，建立了统一处理预应力下振动与屈曲问题的线性化特征值系统。</li>
<li>创新性地应用微分变换法（DTM）于壳体问题，实现了对指定周向波数下连续二维周向模态形状的直接、无网格构造。</li>
<li>系统揭示了预应力引起的周向模态形状演化规律，明确了径向压力与轴向压缩在影响模态分布方面的本质差异。</li></ul>
<h3>局限性</h3>
<ul><li>基于线性化理论，适用于小变形和稳定性分岔点附近的预测，可能无法捕捉后屈曲行为或大振幅非线性振动。</li>
<li>研究主要针对特定边界条件（简支、固支-自由）和薄壳理论，其结论向其他复杂边界或厚壳的推广性有待验证。</li>
<li>未考虑材料非线性、几何初始缺陷以及动态载荷的影响。</li></ul>
<h3>可能的发展方向</h3>

## 摘要

<p>Active stress models in cardiac biomechanics account for the mechanical deformation caused by muscle activity, thus providing a link between the electrophysiological and mechanical properties of the tissue. The accurate assessment of active stress parameters is fundamental for a precise understanding of myocardial function but remains difficult to achieve in a clinical setting, especially when only displacement and strain data from medical imaging modalities are available. This study introduces a novel physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) framework for inferring active contractility in time-dependent cardiac biomechanics from these types of imaging data. By leveraging adaptive weighting schemes, residual-based attention mechanisms, ad-hoc regularisation strategies, Fourier Features, the proposed method achieves robust and accurate reconstruction of active stress fields in various settings, in the presence of noise and with a high spatial resolution. In addition, we perform a thorough Pareto front analysis to study the influence of the loss weights in the reconstruction of active stress parameters. Finally, we apply the method to the characterisation of tissue inhomogeneities and detection of fibrotic scars in myocardial tissue. This work represents a significant methodological advancement, addressing key challenges in inverse problems for computational biomechanics and it opens a new pathway to significantly improve the diagnosis, treatment planning, and management of heart conditions associated with cardiac diseases and conditions associated with tissue heterogeneity, such as cardiac fibrosis or myocardial infarction.</p>

<p><strong>为什么做：</strong>在心脏生物力学中，活性应力模型连接了组织的电生理特性和力学变形。准确评估活性应力参数对于理解心肌功能至关重要，但在临床环境中（通常仅能获得影像学位移/应变数据）难以实现。现有方法在此类数据受限的逆问题上存在挑战，因此需要发展新的、稳健的、高分辨率的参数估计方法，以改善与组织异质性相关的心脏疾病（如心肌纤维化或梗死）的诊断和治疗规划。</p>
<p><strong>怎么做：</strong>1. **理论模型**：基于心脏生物力学的控制方程（包括平衡方程、本构关系，其中包含被动超弹性材料和主动应力贡献），构建PINNs的物理约束损失函数。
2. **网络与优化**：使用神经网络同时近似位移场和活性应力场。采用专门的优化方案，并结合了多种技术提升性能与稳定性，包括：自适应加权方案（adaptive weighting）、基于残差的注意力机制（residual-based attention）、针对活性应力网络的正则化策略（regularisation）、傅里叶特征（Fourier Features）输入编码、精确施加边界条件、以及特定的配点采样策略。
3. **验证与测试**：方法在数值生成的仿真数据上进行测试。首先在均质模型（包括准静态和完全时变模型）中验证，然后在包含单个/多个球形疤痕（含灰度过渡区）的异质模型中进行测试，以评估其检测组织异质性的能力。文中还进行了详尽的帕累托前沿（Pareto front）分析，以研究损失函数权重对重建结果的影响。</p>

<li>该方法对噪声具有鲁棒性，并能实现高空间分辨率的参数重建。</li>
<li>该框架可用于识别心肌组织中的异质性，如纤维化疤痕，展示了其在心脏疾病临床评估中的潜在应用价值。</li></ul>
<h3>亮点和创新点</h3>

<li>集成并发展了多项提升PINNs性能的技术，如自适应加权、残差注意力机制和针对特定参数的正则化策略，以应对该多物理场反问题的复杂性。</li>
<li>成功演示了该方法在检测和表征心肌组织异质性（如疤痕）方面的能力，为无创性心脏功能评估提供了新工具。</li></ul>
<h3>局限性</h3>
